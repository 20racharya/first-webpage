Family

Culture and Background

My parent were born and raised in south India.

My father's family struggled finacially as his parents had as many as seven children, including my dad, to care for. His parents earned money by owning a resteraunt where my father would often help out at when he was young. My father took a liking to computer programming which became the job with the most positions in America and started to travel.

My mother lived in Shimoga which was a city on a high hill in India. She lived with many of her relatives in a small house but enjoyed a carefree life playing around in the forest and jungle terrains that once existed in India.

Although we visited India frequently, the trips were hellish for me. I had a weak constitution and suffered from asthma. The affects of these illnesses would be agravated by the dirty atmosphere in India and I had sleepless night often inhaling a vaporized drug solution in order to breath.
